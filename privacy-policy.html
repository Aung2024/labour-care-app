Privacy Policy

Maternal & Child Health Care System

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Maternal & Child Health Care System (the 'App') collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal and health information. This application processes clinical information for patient care. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your health data in accordance with WHO Digital Health Ethics and Data Governance principles, Myanmar Cybersecurity Law No. 1/2025 (as applicable to digital platforms and critical information systems), your facility's professional confidentiality obligations, and Jhpiego/MOH data governance policies.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide healthcare services:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Patient name, age, and date of birth
  • Contact information (phone number, address)
  • Region, township, and facility information

2.2 Health Information

  • Medical history and records
  • Antenatal care visit records
  • Labour and delivery information
  • Postnatal care records
  • Vaccination records
  • Newborn care information

2.3 User Account Information

  • Healthcare provider email and credentials
  • User role and permissions
  • Activity logs for audit purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage healthcare services
  • To maintain accurate medical records
  • To track patient care progress and outcomes
  • To generate reports for healthcare management and planning
  • To ensure quality of care and patient safety
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data are encrypted and stored securely. Your use of the app is subject to Myanmar's Cybersecurity Law and relevant data protection principles.

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Only authorized healthcare providers can access patient data. You are responsible for securing your device, ensuring no unauthorized person accesses the app.
  • Authentication: All users must log in with secure credentials. Keep your login credentials confidential.
  • Audit Logs: All data access and modifications are logged for security monitoring. Audit logs will record your usage for data security purposes.
  • Data Breach Reporting: Any suspected data breach must be reported immediately to your supervisor and system administrator.
  • Confidentiality: Patient data is confidential and must not be disclosed except for clinical care or authorized reporting.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Your health information is stored securely on Google Firebase cloud servers, which comply with international security and privacy standards. Data is stored in encrypted format and backed up regularly.

We retain your health records in accordance with legal requirements and healthcare best practices. Records are typically retained for a minimum period as required by law to ensure continuity of care and for legal compliance.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your health information:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your health records
  • Right to Correction: You can request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time (subject to legal requirements)
  • Right to Complain: You can file a complaint if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

To exercise these rights, please contact your healthcare provider or the system administrator.

7. Consent

By using this App, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your health information as described in this Privacy Policy. Your consent is required before we can collect and process your health data.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting your healthcare provider. However, withdrawal of consent may affect our ability to provide healthcare services.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through the App or via email. The 'Last Updated' date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
