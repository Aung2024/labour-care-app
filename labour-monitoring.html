Partograph Guidelines

Key Monitoring Parameters
Fetal Parameters:
  • Fetal heart rate
  • Amniotic fluid (color)
  • Moulding of fetal head
  • Descent of presenting part
Maternal Parameters:
  • Cervical dilation
  • Uterine contractions
  • Temperature
  • Pulse & Blood pressure
  • Urine output

How to Use Monitoring Tools

  1. Start Labour Care: Go to Labour Care section and select active labour patient
  2. Enter Summary: Click "Continue Care" to open the WHO Labour Care Summary
  3. Record Data: Input vital signs, contractions, and fetal monitoring data
  4. Monitor Progress: Use partograph to track labour progression
  5. View Charts: Use "View Summary" for comprehensive visualization
  6. Take Action: Follow alert lines and protocols for interventions
